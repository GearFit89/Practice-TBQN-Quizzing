How It Works

Ready to play? It's as easy as 1-2-3.

1. Set Up Your Game

First, pick how you want to play. Choose your quiz mode, how many questions you want, and get things ready for a fun round.

2. Pick Your Verses

Select the specific months and "flights" you want to focus on. This lets you practice the exact verses that matter most to your group.

3. Time to Play!

Once everything's set, hit "Start." Verses will pop up based on your choices. Just type your answer and see how you do!

4. Check Your Score

Answers all have to be word perfect but there is a built in spell checker for that. As soon as you type a wrong word in a verse you wil be prompted to "please correct"

Your Progress is Safe with Us

We save your progress right on your device. Here's how it works.

Client-Side Storage

The Bible Quizzing Companion uses your browser's local storage to keep a record of how you're doing. This data is **private and only stored on your device**. You can track your progress without creating an account or sharing any personal info.

  • When you answer, the app updates a simple counter for each verse.
  • This lets the app show you which verses you're acing and which ones you need to work on.
  • Since everything is saved locally, clearing your browser's cache or using a different device will reset your progress.
